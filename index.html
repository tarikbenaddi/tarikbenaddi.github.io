Bernard Werber — French writer
I received an electrical engineering degree in telecommunications and networks from ENSEEIHT and research master in Telecommunications from INP Toulouse, both in October 2012. I then conducted my Ph.D (funded by the French Space Agency (CNES and Thales Alenia Space compagny) in “Sparce graph-based coding schemes for continuous phase modulations” within the Signal and Communication Group of the Institut de Recherche en Informatique de Toulouse (IRIT). In 2016, I joined IMT Atlantique as an assistant professor. Since 2020, I am an R&D system engineer in satellites optical communications at Thales Alenia Space.
